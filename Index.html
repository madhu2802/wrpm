We Worship

Worship is the fuel for disciplineship. We are called to speak, think and work to the glory of God. We worship God by remembering the gospel through preaching.

We belong

Community is the context for discipleinship. The Christian Faith is not intended to be lived in isolation, we were made for relationship with god and for relationship with each other. We belong not merely to a local church, but to the people.

We Serve

Service is the overflow of disciplineship. It seeks to extend hod's grace and mercy to others for his glory and not our own. We serve as an expression of love and stewardship of grace.

We Multiply

Multiplication is the result of disciplineship. We become better disciples through love for God and love for others by equipping and empowering others to do the same. We multiply the kingdom by living with the purpose to make gog known and enjoyed.
